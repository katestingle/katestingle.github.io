01 — About

Hi, I'm Kate.

I'm a Computer Engineering student at Cal Poly San Luis Obispo, expecting to graduate in 2028. My focus areas live at the intersection of embedded systems, satellite communications, and software-defined radio — basically, anything that involves getting hardware and signals to talk to each other.

At PolySat, Cal Poly's CubeSat laboratory, I write flight software and operate ground stations for real satellite missions — everything from Linux driver development to hardware-in-the-loop testing on the lab bench.

Outside the lab, you'll find me on the air. I hold a General Class FCC amateur radio license, lead emergency communications for the Cal Poly Amateur Radio Club, and train new hams across Southern California through GLAARG and CPARC exam sessions.
